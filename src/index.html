LC is a loose collective of researchers, artists, and activists working to liberate computing from capitalism.

We live under a capitalist mode of computing. The tools, languages, techniques, and assumptions of digital systems and structured by economic forces that shape not just what we can do, but what we can imagine doing. By separating production from use, producing inflexible software, and slicing up computing into siloed apps, your agency is held back by a tech industry that benefits from a population rendered computationally passive.

We seek to catalyze discontent with the state of computing and firmly believe that a better computing is possible. Only by understanding the history of paths-not-taken and closely examining processes of production, labor conditions, power structures, and ideological influences, can we understand the winding path towards a liberatory computing.
